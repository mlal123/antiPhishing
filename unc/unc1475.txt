Hello Mangza,

 

Your room selection portal timeslot will open at 2/18/2016 10:10:00 AM.

 

Please follow these steps to complete your room selection:

 

1.     
Log in to your 2016-2017 application via the MyHousing Portal

2.     
Once directed to the Room Selection page, click the
Residence Hall Room Selection or Apartment Room Selection link

3.     
You will be given a list of buildings in which you are eligible to live

4.     
Click a building name to view available spaces

5.     
When you find a room you’d like to select, click the room number, then Confirm

6.     
Follow the directions to select it and pull in your roommates to their spaces (if applicable).


 

You will be given 5 minutes to complete this process.

 

Tips:

·        
Anyone in your roommate group can select the assignment for the entire roommate group. When selecting the assignment, this person will choose a bed space for each person in the group.
o  
For example: If you have 6 people in a group, anyone in the group can assign the group members. Once a room or suite is selected, the group member will assign each person in the group to a specific bedroom within a suite. This
 gives each group the ability to match up roommates within the suite.
·        
You must assign everyone in the roommate group to an assignment.
o  
For example: You cannot have 8 people in a roommate group and sign up for a 6 person Super Suite.
·        
Once you are assigned to a room, you cannot change your assignment until the room refinement period that runs from February 22 – March 18.
·        
Only available rooms will show up in the room selection process.

o  
For example: If you are looking for single or an apartment and there are none showing up, then there are no more available. Please do not call the Housing office to see if anymore will open up.

Once you are finished, you should be able to view your assignment and roommate information by clicking the Application Status and Assignment Details link at the top right side of the page.


 

If you have any difficulties during the selection process, please contact us at 919-962-5401. Any questions or issues that arise after you complete selection can be addressed to
housing@unc.edu.

 

Best of luck, and thank you for choosing on-campus housing to be your home for the coming year!

 

Department of Housing and Residential Education
