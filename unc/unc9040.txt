Dear Mangza
 
 
This is a reminder to apply to the 2018 Harvey Beech Scholarship. Applications are now available online at
https://diversity.unc.edu/2018-unc-harvey-beech-scholarship/
     

Applications and supporting materials are due by
Thursday, April 12, 2018. All supporting material (recommendation letter and unofficial transcript) can be dropped off at 09 South Building to Taneisha Henderson, University Office for Diversity & Inclusion, CB #9125, by the deadline.

 
Please note- Your application will not be considered without the supporting documentation.

 
About the Scholarship: Named for the late Harvey
 Beech, the first African-American student to graduate from UNC, the awards have been given annually to Carolina junior and sophomores since 1989 to recognize community leadership and academic scholarship.
 
Awardees are chosen based on academic GPA improvement (semester-based or cumulative), financial need, and contributions to campus life.
 The Scholarship Committee awards $1,500 scholarships to three students annually. The scholarships are open to all sophomores and juniors at UNC-Chapel Hill.
     

Contact Sophia McFarlane at
sophscm@live.unc.edu for further information or read the
FAQs.
