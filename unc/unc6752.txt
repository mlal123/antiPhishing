15 hours/week

The Odum Institute seeks to hire a UNC graduate student to assist with survey research projects beginning immediately. The assistant will work 15
hours per week on a regular weekly schedule in the Odum Institute offices in Davis Library during normal 9am-6pm M-F business hours. Projects include
mail and web surveys. Nearly all work of this position must be completed in person; remote access or nontraditional work hours will not be adequate.

Example duties include:
• Assisting with design and preparation of questionnaires and other survey materials
• Pretesting questionnaires and/or cognitive interviewing
• Overseeing/coordinating mailings for mail surveys
• Overseeing editing and data entry of completed questionnaires
• Managing returned mail
• Creating weekly data collection status reports
• Qualtrics programming
• Distributing web survey invitations and reminders
• Assisting with data file preparation
• Basic statistical analysis and report writing
Required skills/experience:
• Keen attention to detail
• Understanding of quantitative research methods and importance of standardization and data quality
• Reliable attendance with minimal absences
• Willingness to ask questions and seek clarification when needed
• Focus on thoroughness and documentation of study design decisions
• Basic Excel worksheet and data manipulation skills
Preferred skills/experience:
• Survey methodology coursework and/or experience
• Data coding and/or data entry experience
• Qualtrics programming
• Basic SAS or SPSS skills
This is an hourly position available only to UNC students. No tuition remission is provided. Interested students please email cover letter and resume
to Teresa_Edwards@unc.edu ( mailto:Teresa_Edwards@unc.edu ).
