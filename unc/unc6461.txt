Dear second- and third-year students,

If you are pursuing a career in mathematics, the natural sciences, or engin=
eering, you should consider applying for the Goldwater Scholarship!

The Barry M. Goldwater Scholarship is a merit-based award for current secon=
d- and third-year students who have outstanding potential and intend to pur=
sue careers in mathematics, the natural sciences, or engineering. The schol=
arship provides students with up to $7,500 per academic year to cover the c=
osts of tuition, room, board, books, and fees.

To apply for this award, you must first be nominated by a faculty member by=
 Thursday, October 5, 2017. You may ask a faculty member to nominate you. N=
ominations should be sent from faculty to ods@unc.edu a=
nd should include the following information: Student's name, Student's PID,=
 Student's UNC email address, Nominator's name, and Nominator's email addre=
ss.

Once nominated, you will be invited to apply for endorsement through the Of=
fice of Distinguished Scholarships. The application includes a 2-page essay=
 and 3 letters of recommendation. UNC is eligible to endorse up to 4 nomina=
ted students to compete for up to 300 Goldwater scholarships nationally. UN=
C has had a total of 47 Goldwater Scholars, including 2 last year.

Eligibility Requirements:


*              Open to current second- and third-year students intending to=
 pursue a career in math, science, or engineering.

*              Scholarships are not available for students who intend to pu=
rsue a career in actuarial science or for students who intend to attend med=
ical school to become a medical doctor in private practice.  However, stude=
nts attending medical school who intend to pursue a research career are eli=
gible.

*              Students must be a United States citizen or a resident alien=
.

*              Students normally have a GPA of 3.6 or above.

For more information about the Goldwater, visit ODS's website here, Goldw=
ater's website here, or email ods@unc=
.edu.

We hope you consider applying!
