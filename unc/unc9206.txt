Tar Heel Fans,


The third annual Legacy Golf Tournament will be held on Friday, May 18th at the award-winning, Tom Fazio-designed UNC Finley Golf Course.

 

The Legacy celebrates Carolina's rich sports history by bringing back Tar Heel greats from the past. Players and coaches representing a variety of UNC sports will join participating foursomes and compete for a spot in a playoff that immediately follows play.


Legacy players scheduled to attend include Antawn Jamison, Phil Ford, Dave Hanners, Anson Dorrance, Karen Shelton, Scott Bankhead, B.J. Surhoff and more.

 

The tournament entry fee is $1,600 per foursome and includes golf, complimentary food and beverages, a custom Titleist duffel bag and a variety of gifts. Proceeds will benefit Carolina Athletics.
