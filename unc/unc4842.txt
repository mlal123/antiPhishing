Next week we will celebrate Women in Tech with events on Monday, Wednesday,
Thursday (courtesy of GWiCS)  and Friday. This is the brainchild of Kaylee

*Monday, 3/27*

*All Day  * #IStandWithWomenInTech poster and photo opportunities


*Wednesday, 3/29*

*5:00 p.m.  * Film Screening: *She Started It: A Documentary on Women Tech
Founders*

*6:45 p.m.  * Women in Tech Panel


*Thursday, 3/30*

*6:30 p.m.  * GWiCS Poster & Demo Session (Registration required:
bit.ly/2nO0udE)


*Friday, 3/31*

*10 a.m. - 2 p.m.  * Famous women in tech trivia. Enter to win a prize.


More information is available at https://cs.unc.edu/event/
women-in-tech-week-2017/.
