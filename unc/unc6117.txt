Student employees of UNC-Chapel Hill are subject to the same email policy requirements as full-time faculty and staff. Two primary rules employees
must follow based on the Email Address Policy (
https://its.unc.edu/?email_id=17&user_id=32044&urlpassed=aHR0cHM6Ly9pdHMudW5jLmVkdS8%2FZW1haWxfaWQ9MTcmYW1wO3VzZXJfaWQ9MTEmYW1wO3VybHBhc3NlZD1hSFIwY0RvdkwybDBjeTUxYm1NdVpXUjFMMlpwYkdWekx6SXdNVFF2TURndlpXMWhhV3d0WVdSa2NtVnpjeTF3YjJ4cFkza3VjR1JtJmFtcDtjb250cm9sbGVyPXN0YXRzJmFtcDthY3Rpb249YW5hbHlzZSZhbXA7d3lzaWphLXBhZ2U9MSZhbXA7d3lzaWphcD1zdWJzY3JpcHRpb25z&controller=stats&action=analyse&wysija-page=1&wysijap=subscriptions
) are as follows: 
First, use ONLY University email accounts for University business. Use of a personal email account for University business is a violation of the
UNC-Chapel Hill Email Address Policy (
https://its.unc.edu/?email_id=17&user_id=32044&urlpassed=aHR0cHM6Ly9pdHMudW5jLmVkdS8%2FZW1haWxfaWQ9MTcmYW1wO3VzZXJfaWQ9MTEmYW1wO3VybHBhc3NlZD1hSFIwY0RvdkwybDBjeTUxYm1NdVpXUjFMMlpwYkdWekx6SXdNVFF2TURndlpXMWhhV3d0WVdSa2NtVnpjeTF3YjJ4cFkza3VjR1JtJmFtcDtjb250cm9sbGVyPXN0YXRzJmFtcDthY3Rpb249YW5hbHlzZSZhbXA7d3lzaWphLXBhZ2U9MSZhbXA7d3lzaWphcD1zdWJzY3JpcHRpb25z&controller=stats&action=analyse&wysija-page=1&wysijap=subscriptions
). 
Second, also based on the above policy, employees are not allowed to auto-forward UNC-Chapel Hill email to an email system that is not run by or
authorized by UNC-Chapel Hill. Auto-forwarding of email may put University-owned or managed information at risk. If you (as an employee) have set your
UNC-Chapel Hill email account to auto-forward to an email address not ending in unc.edu or not an authorized affiliate institution, please remove your
auto-forward. Information Technology Services (ITS) plans to run an automated process periodically to remove inappropriate auto-forwarding by
employees of UNC-Chapel Hill. 
If you have questions about email appropriate use, please contact help.unc.edu or call 919-962-HELP at any time for consultation or referral to
appropriate parties. A relevant help document (
https://its.unc.edu/?email_id=17&user_id=32044&urlpassed=aHR0cDovL2hlbHAudW5jLmVkdS9oZWxwL2F1dG8tZm9yd2FyZGluZy1vZi1lbWFpbC10by11bnRydXN0ZWQtZW1haWwtZG9tYWlucy8%3D&controller=stats&action=analyse&wysija-page=1&wysijap=subscriptions
) has been posted at help.unc.edu for your convenience.
