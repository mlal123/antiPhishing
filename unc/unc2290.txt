*NSF Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) Program*

 *Making Science Visible: Using Visualization Technology to Support
Linguistically Diverse Middle School Students' Learning in Physical and
Life Sciences (NSF DRL-1552114)*

 Principal Investigator: Dr. Kelly Ryoo
, School of Education,
UNC-CH (khryoo@email.unc.edu)

 *Application Deadline: August 14, 2016*

My name is Kelly Ryoo. I am a faculty member in the School of Education. We
are looking for a highly motivated undergraduate student majoring in
Computer Science who is interested in conducting *research* under *the **NSF
Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) program*. The selected
student will receive *$8,120 in total stipends* with the potential for
extension for next year. *Students from underrepresented minority groups
are especially encouraged to apply.*
