Global Take Off: Puerto Rico | Go Global for Free
 
Are you starting your first year at UNC this fall? Interested in traveling, but not sure how to pay for it or fit it in your schedule? The Center for Global Initiatives (CGI)

 Applications are due next Friday, June 30, 2017. Visit CGI's website to
