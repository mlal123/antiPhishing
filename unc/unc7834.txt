Good evening Student Staff,
 
Happy closing season… We’re almost at the break! Within this email, you will find a link for SignUp.com to sign up for Mid-Year Training sessions on Friday January 5th. Please do one of the two following options when you sign up:
 
·      
You can sign up for one certificate session. If you do this, this will be the
ONLY session you sign up for. You will notice that it will go the entire time of the training (1:30pm-4:50pm). These include:

o  
One Act Training

o  
Haven Skills Training

o  
Green Zone Training

o  
Safe Zone Training
 
·      
If you do not sign up for a certificate session, you will sign up for a session during these time blocks below. You will sign up for
one session during EACH of these time frames:

o  
1:30pm-2:30pm

o  
2:40pm-3:40pm

o  
3:50pm-4:50pm

o  
*** There is a MakerSpace orientation during each time frame. If you choose to go to one of those sessions, you only go to the one you signed up for. They are all the same orientation just offered at different times.
 
While signing up, you will have the opportunity to browse through the sessions that are offered and read about what each session will entail and who will be presenting it. As a committee, we have worked really hard to take RA feedback from
 last year to make sure you all are being offered the types of presentations you felt you needed the most.
 
Signups for sessions will 
close on Monday December 18th at 12pm.
 
“What will happen if I don’t sign up in time?” – if you do not sign up in time, you will be automatically placed in sessions based on what is available. There will not be any exceptions allowed to this and you will not be
 able to switch out of the assigned sessions.
 
Link for the sign-up: 
http://signup.com/go/LSZgDvo
·      
You do NOT need to create an account and password to sign up for this .
 
If you have any questions about the sign up process, please feel free to reach out to me. I’ll be happy to help as much as I can.
