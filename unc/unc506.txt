Dear Mangza, 

The Real-U Study will soon come to a close, but there are still opportunities to join in the coming weeks.
Just to remind you, the Real-U study is a campus-wide research study designed to help us learn more about lives and experiences of UNC students.  Participants complete two surveys
 and a brief lab task administered in two testing sessions, separated by two weeks, at the Center of Developmental Science located in the Top of the Hill Building in downtown Chapel Hill.

 
The surveys will ask about a wide range of experiences unique to college students such as use of social media, relationships with friends and family, health behaviors and well-being,
 and substance use. Participants completing both testing sessions will receive $45 and have an opportunity to enter a drawing for an additional $100.

 
If you are interested in participating, please visit our research portal --
https://cdsresearchportal.sona-systems.com
– to complete a brief pre-screen and to register for the study. For more information, visit our website
www.realu.web.unc.edu
or email us at
realustudy@unc.edu.

 
 
Thank you!
 
 
Andrea Hussong, Ph.D.                                                          Sarah Morgan, M.S.W.

Director, Center for Developmental Science                                     REAL-U, Project Director

 
 
The research study is IRB # 14-0360.
