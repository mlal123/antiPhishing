The Town of Chapel Hill Parks and Recreation Department is supporting Carolina Basketball and the North Carolina Disaster Relief Fund with the #RodeoOnRosemary Fall Food Truck Rodeo starting at 12:00 p.m. on Sunday on Rosemary Street in Chapel Hill.   Bring the kids and enjoy the some pre-game festivities with giant games, a miniature golf course on The Plaza, a live DJ spinning your favorite tunes and more than a dozen of finest local food trucks before the game on Sunday then head to the Disaster Relief Jamboree!
