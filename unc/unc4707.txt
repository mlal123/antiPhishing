We are pleased to announce that we are currently accepting applications from UNC
Chapel Hill undergraduate students to serve as official ambassadors for the UNC
Business Essentials program during the next academic year.  Student Ambassadors will
each receive full scholarship for the UNC Business Essentials program (a $2,500 value).

To be considered, you should submit a 250 word essay describing why you should be
selected as an ambassador and how you would help to promote the program here at
UNC Chapel Hill.  Essays must be submitted by March 19st, 2017.  After essays have
been received and reviewed, select candidates will be invited to interview for a limited
number of ambassador positions. If selected as an ambassador, you must complete the
online business certificate before September 1, 2017.  Upon completion of the
certificate program, ambassadors will serve as campus liaisons, helping to promote
UNC Business Essentials throughout the upcoming academic year, and building
awareness through social media and campus events. Please go to our website for more
details.

UNC Business Essentials was designed by Kenan-Flagler faculty to teach business
basics to individuals who have chosen non-business majors or who want to improve
their understanding of the business fundamentals through the following six online
modules:

·         Introduction to Business
·         Marketing
·         Financial Accounting
·         Business Operations
·         Economics and Finance
·         Business Communication

The online format allows you to work at your own pace from anywhere in the world, as
you gain a solid foundation in business knowledge to jump start your career.
