From Side Project to 25 Million Downloads
-----------------------------------------

In 2011, Ryan joined Codecademy and used what he learned to
turn his app idea into reality. Five years later, his side
project has been downloaded 25 million times and Ryan is now the
VP of Product & Engineering with the company that acquired his
app.

See the full story and learn how Ryan's app Sworkit ( https://sworkit.com/?utm_source=codecademy&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=codecademy_story ) was:

* featured in publications like Lifehacker, Mashable, and
Elle
* acquired by another health startup
* pitched on the TV show Shark Tank ( http://www.businessinsider.com/how-to-use-sworkit-best-workout-app-that-was-on-shark-tank )

This month, Sworkit hit three straight months of profitability, a
massive milestone in the startup lifecycle. Congratulations to
Ryan and the team at Sworkit!

This Month in Tech: Ten Years of Scratch
----------------------------------------

Scratch - the free, fun, visual programming language
designed by MIT for children between 6 and 16 years of age -
turns ten years old this month!

Read about Scratch ( https://backchannel.com/the-kids-computer-language-that-became-a-mind-bomb-for-the-hacker-ethic-a0b7e42c229d?utm_source=codecademy_newsletter ) or see it for yourself ( https://scratch.mit.edu/about?utm_source=codecademy_newsletter ).
