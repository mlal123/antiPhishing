BREAKING: Silent Sam painted red, protester arrested
==========

Doctoral student Maya Little painted Silent Sam red ( https://sable.madmimi.com/c/14086?id=2492994.42003.1.bd6758468361afaa441e72e9355f402c ) on Monday afternoon during a protest and was subsequently arrested by UNC Police. The statue has since been cleaned, and Little has released a statement to The Daily Tar Heel.
